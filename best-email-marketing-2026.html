FAQ

What is the best email marketing platform in 2026?
Loops leads with a 4.8/5 rating. For free plans, Loops is top-rated with a generous free tier.
How much does email marketing software cost?
Pricing varies by subscriber count. Most platforms offer free plans up to 500-1000 subscribers. Paid plans typically range from $9-50/month for up to 5,000 subscribers.
Which email marketing tool is best for e-commerce?
Klaviyo and Omnisend are specifically designed for e-commerce with Shopify/WooCommerce integrations and revenue-focused automations.
What email marketing platform is best for creators?
Kit (ConvertKit) and beehiiv are built for creators and newsletters. beehiiv has a strong free plan and is particularly popular for newsletter monetization.
