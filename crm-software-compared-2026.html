FAQ

What is the best CRM software in 2026?
Based on user ratings, Less Annoying CRM (4.8/5) leads our CRM comparison. For small businesses, free options like HubSpot, Zoho CRM, and Freshsales are excellent starting points.
What CRM has the best free plan?
HubSpot offers one of the most generous free CRM plans with unlimited users and contacts. Zoho CRM Free supports 3 users. Freshsales Free is also strong for small teams.
When should a business switch from spreadsheets to a CRM?
When you have more than 50 active deals/contacts, a dedicated CRM pays off. The average CRM user reports 30-40% improvement in deal close rates.
What is the cheapest paid CRM?
Among our tracked tools, monday CRM starts at $12/month.
