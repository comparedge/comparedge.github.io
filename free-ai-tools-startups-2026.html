FAQ

What free AI tools do startups actually need?
The most valuable free AI tools for startups are: AI writing assistant (for content/emails), AI coding tool (to accelerate development), AI image generator (for visual content), and a free CRM with AI features.
Are free AI tools really free?
The tools in this list all have permanent free plans (not just trials). However, free plans have limits — typically on usage volume, features, or seats. Most startups can start on free plans and upgrade as they grow.
Which free AI tool has the best ROI for startups?
AI coding assistants (GitHub Copilot free, Cursor free) typically offer the highest ROI — even a 10% productivity boost for a 2-person dev team can save thousands of dollars monthly.
